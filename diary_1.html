"In Clorox We Wipe."

During the flu season, my boss handed me Clorox wipes. He told me to wipe down every inch of the computer and keyboard every hour.

“Germs everywhere! I don't want to get sick!“, he said.

The following day, we had to sign a birthday card for one of the employees. I happened to be the last person signing, so I had to seal it. I tried to find tape or glue. I checked every drawer but I couldn’t find anything. I knocked on my boss’s office.

“Do you have some glue?” I asked.
He looked at me for a second and laughed.

“Oh, you don’t need glue!”
He licked the envelope, then handed the card back to me.

“There you go!” He smiled.

. . . I didn't know what to do with it.
