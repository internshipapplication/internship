"If I ever get a pet moose,
I will name him Tillamook."

There are so many options you can choose in the grocery stores nowadays, especially cheese. I grew up eating plastic textured orange squares, thinking I don’t really like cheese. When I first had my sharp cheddar, I got hooked right away.

Apparently, Fragments of cheese protein, called casomorphins, attach to the same brain receptors as heroin and other narcotics. As a result, each bite of cheese produces a tiny hit of dopamine.

" Hi, my name is Hahn, and I’m addicted to cheese."
