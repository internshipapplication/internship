"Super Bowl 2020"


I don’t understand American Football. Why do they call it 'Football' when most of the players use their hands ? ! You only use your feet when you kick a ball.

There are so many rules too. Americans make self-deprecating jokes about themselves being stupid, but I feel like they should be proud that they understand this complicated sport.

Though, I love the hot wings and funny commercials, so it sort of balances out.
