"Dome Home Gnome Gnome Gnome"


“I want to live in a monolithic Dome Home.” He said.

“Ummm… like Telletubbies?” I asked.

“Where do you want to live in then, Hahn?” He asked.
“Err . . . The most generic suburban house you can think of, with a white dog named Sam.” I said.

Right after I answered, I realized that is my American dream. Having a dog, a house, and shopping at Whole Foods without worrying about the price tag.

. . . One day , when the dog gets older than me in dog years, he will be Uncle Sam.
